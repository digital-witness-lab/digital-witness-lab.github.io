WHO WE ARE

Digital Witness Lab is a non-profit research initiative that collects data to expose surveillance, misinformation, and other harms on digital platforms. We build independent, public, and open-source resources to scrutinize data-driven technologies so we can demand accountability for their harms. The Lab is hosted by the Center for Information Technology Policy (CITP) at Princeton University.

JOB PURPOSE

The Research Associate will support the India Research Lead in carrying out data-driven investigations by conducting field surveys, data collection, and data labeling for the Digital Witness Lab's various projects, including the flagship WhatsApp Watch, which looks at the impact of digital platforms on society.

WHAT YOU WILL DO

  • Provide support to India Research Lead in designing and executing field surveys and collect data on the use and impact of digital platforms.
  • Support the team's data annotation efforts by enriching data with local context.
  • Conduct data-driven investigations and periodically write articles/research reports on the emerging trends.
  • Provide support to other teams at Digital Witness Lab in building structured databases and novel datasets on the use and impact of digital platforms.
  • Periodically contribute to newsletters and other public-facing output based on data research.

APPLY IF THIS IS YOU

  • Have at least four years of experience as a journalist or researcher.
  • Have demonstrable experience in conducting data-driven investigations and/or field surveys at the intersection of technology, society, and politics.
  • Have prior experience or knowledge of policies concerning the use of personal data, digital platforms, and political and social communication.
  • Have excellent writing, editing, and communication skills in English. (Proficiency in any other language spoken in India would be highly preferred.)

SKILLS THAT'LL BOOST YOUR CHANCES

  • Working knowledge of data analysis software, such as MS Excel, Google Sheets, Airtable, or SQL.
  • Comfortable working with legal/policy documents.
  • Habit of reading/following news and brimming with ideas.
